About EduSchedule

The idea for EduSchedule was born when three seniors at Glenbrook South High School - Michael, Thomas, and Daniel - realized the inefficiency involved in scheduling meetings with their teachers. They had all experienced the cumbersome process of email exchanges or Google Calendar appointments while trying to coordinate a meeting with a teacher. Brainstorming ideas, they also learned that teachers, counselors, and tutors faced the same issues. EduSchedule, a simple and modern web app for managing appointments, is the solution they came up with.

EduSchedule features a simple sign-up process with an equally simple process for registering an institution. The application includes an easy-to-use appointment booking and management interface, providing options to edit and cancel appointments. Users receive notifications and can customize them based on their preferences. And, for institution administrators, EduSchedule integrates easily with existing technology, requiring only an email address for end-user registration.

The software is open source and freely-licensed under the GNU GPLv3. The code is available at https://github.com/michaelpolinski/EduSchedule.
